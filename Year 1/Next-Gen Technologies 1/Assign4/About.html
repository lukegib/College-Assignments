The Who, What, When & Where of Bulby

Bulby is a world-wide company dedicated to providing the world with the best bulbs around. We care about the earth and its environment deeply here at Bulby. To honor our commitment to the earth Bulby goes out of it's way to create the most energy-efficient bulbs and make them accessible to everyone on the planet. Bulby was Founded in 2018 by President and CEO of Bulby, Luke Gibbons. Luke's goal with Bulby is to make lives brighter whilst restoring the environment. Bulbys main office is situated in the heart of Galway City open to all who would like to contact us.
